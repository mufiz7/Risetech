Aruba Instant On AP22

The Aruba Instant On AP22 is a Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) indoor access point designed by Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) specifically for small businesses and high-density environments like offices, schools, hotels, and cafes. It aims to provide fast, reliable, and secure Wi-Fi without the complexity and cost associated with enterprise-level solutions.

A compact, Wi‑Fi 6 certified (802.11ax) indoor access point ideal for small businesses, offices, schools, and retailers. Offers easy setup, cloud app management, and high performance in a sleek, low-profile design.

Key Features

  • Wi‑Fi 6 (2×2:2) – Dual-band access with max 1.7 Gbps aggregate throughput, backward-compatible with older Wi‑Fi standards
  • Efficiency Enhancements – Supports OFDMA, MU‑MIMO, 1024‑QAM, Target Wake Time to improve capacity, reduce latency, and conserve device battery life
  • SmartMesh Support – Wirelessly extend coverage with multiple APs, no extra cloud controller required
  • Built-in Security – Includes WPA2/3, OWE guest network, firewall gateway, TPM-based credential protection, and two-factor authentication
  • App & Web Management – Configure via Instant On app or cloud portal; zero licensing fees, 24/7 support
  • Integrated Bluetooth – Enables beaconing, IoT support, and streamlined device commissioning
  • Advanced Cellular Coexistence – Minimizes interference from nearby LTE/5G signals with filtering
  • Performance – Hands-On – Independent tests show 10–20% faster speed vs Wi‑Fi 5 APs; “extremely easy to set up” users rate it 10/10

    • Specs Overview

  • 2×2 MIMO antennas (2.4 & 5 GHz)
  • Up to 75 clients per AP, 1 GbE PoE (802.3af, 10.1W) or 12V DC
  • Compact size (≈160 × 160 × 37 mm) and light (≈360 g)
  • 2‑year warranty with 24/7 support
