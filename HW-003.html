Huawei MateBook X Pro

The Huawei MateBook X Pro is a premium ultrabook series known for its sleek design, vibrant displays, and powerful performance. The latest models, like the 2024 and 2023 editions, offer cutting-edge features and cater to professionals, creators, and users seeking a high-end computing experience.

A premium ultraportable laptop that combines stunning design with top-tier performance in an ultralight 980 g (≈2.16 lb) chassis

Key Features

  • Display: 14.2″ flexible OLED touchscreen at 3.1K (3120×2080), 120 Hz refresh, 1000 nits peak brightness, 93% screen-to-body
  • Processor & Graphics: Intel Core Ultra 9/7 (185H/155H) with Intel Arc integrated graphics
  • Memory & Storage: Up to 32GB DDR5 and 2TB PCIe Gen4 SSD
  • Battery & Charging: Large 70 Wh battery, lasting ~10 hrs, charges quickly with 90 W USB‑C SuperCharge Turbo
  • Ports: Features 3× USB‑C (2× Thunderbolt 4), supporting charging and DisplayPort
  • Build & Design: Slim 13.5 mm, all-metal body in Space Gray/Ink Blue/White, weighing just 980 g
  • Extras: Intel Evo-certified, fast fingerprint & IR login, top-tier keyboard/touchpad, followed by Dolby Atmos audio, and Huawei’s Super Device features
